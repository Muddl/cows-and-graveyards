# Product Guidelines

## Voice and Tone

**Friendly and approachable.** The game is meant for families and casual players. All text — UI labels, notifications, documentation — should feel warm, inviting, and easy to understand.

## Design Principles

### Simplicity Over Features

- Every feature must justify its complexity
- When in doubt, leave it out
- Fewer taps to accomplish any action is always better
- Avoid settings and options that most users won't need

### Mobile-First

- All interactions designed for touch and thumbs
- Large tap targets, clear visual hierarchy
- Works in portrait orientation for one-handed use
- Responsive to different screen sizes

### Delight

- Visual and audio feedback should feel satisfying
- Celebrate milestones and achievements
- Keep the experience lighthearted
